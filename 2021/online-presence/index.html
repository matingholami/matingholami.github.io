Online Persona

and why does it keep me awake at night?

Published 2021-June-25 by MatinGholami

This post could be read in 2 min

What is Persona?

You can read about Persona on WikiPedia. Persona is the social role one might adopt (defining one’s boundaries, privileges and responsibilities); Persona is the place one is expected to posses in society (by themselves and/or by others).

Think of persona as a mask we put on to show people what we want them to see, or in some cases a mask society puts on our face to make us show what they want to see.

What is online persona?

Internet was first branded as a tool to produce (Really?), share and access Knowledge! People who developed, deployed and invented the Internet would most likely not be able to predict today! Internet nowadays is a global village, a huge one indeed! Internet is a huge network that brings everyone around and every character in this network is a person and persons have personas!

“But how and why?” someone says. well it’s obvious! you have a society of characters of all sorts (some good intentioned, some evilized, some racist, some boringly nice, some big brainers, etc.) that have relations of all sorts and sometimes hierarchies (yes I’m talking of them moderators Putas) thus… Personas are made!

Okay I see that I can be confusing with words sometimes, let’s keep it simple then! we meet people on internet, talk to them, love/hate them, make bonds with them and generally live with them online. we live online! we have a persona online :)

OK! So what?

My point is… Virtual persona of a human being might (and in most cases will) not match the persona used by them in the physical reality! after all the cyber and physical worlds are two parallel universes with different sets of rules and different possibilities.

People like calling it the The virtual persona as if it would become any less real LOL.

In this (so called virtual) society (which is as real as my neighbor who works in a mine) has good people and bad people, slaves and masters, ETC.
